Contribute

We are very interested in hearing about more museum digital projects, which were launched during the lockdowns. If you wish to add your institution - or other examples you think should be added to this map - please, do let us know.
The links below allow you to add data by responding to a short survey: we are providing them in a few different European languages, although we welcome contributions from all over the world. By clicking on the link to the survey (in your chosen language), you will be redirected to a Google Form.
Each survey corresponds to a museum digital project; if you wish to add more than one project, you will be able to submit another response.
